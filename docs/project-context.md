# Hogar Nazareth Web Platform
Project Context Document

## Project Overview

This project consists of the redesign and redevelopment of the website for the **Fundación Hogar del Anciano Nazareth**, a non-profit organization dedicated to the care and support of elderly people.

The goal of the project is to transform the current static website into a **modern, dynamic, and easily manageable platform** that allows the foundation staff to publish content without requiring technical knowledge.

The new platform will improve:

- Institutional visibility
- Communication with the community
- Transparency of information
- Promotion of activities and social work
- Accessibility of information for donors and visitors

This document provides **general context of the project for development tools and AI assistants (such as GitHub Copilot)**.

Technical architecture will be defined in separate documents.

# Organization Background

Organization: **Fundación Hogar del Anciano Nazareth**

Type: **Non-profit social foundation**

Mission:

Provide care, support, and dignity to elderly individuals who require assistance, shelter, and companionship.

Main activities include:

- Housing and caring for elderly residents
- Recreational and social activities
- Physiotherapy and support services
- Community and volunteer engagement
- Donation campaigns

# Content Types

The platform should support the following types of content.

## Institutional Pages

Examples:

- About the foundation
- Mission and vision
- History
- Services

## Activities / News

Posts describing activities organized by the foundation.

Examples:

- Recreational activities
- Celebrations
- Volunteer activities
- Community visits

Each activity may contain:

- Title
- Description
- Images
- Publication date

## Photo Galleries

Collections of photos documenting activities.

Examples:

- Activities by year
- Physiotherapy sessions
- Special celebrations

## Events

Upcoming or past events organized by the foundation.

Examples:

- Community events
- Volunteer days
- Celebrations

## Transparency Documents

Public institutional documents required for transparency.

Examples:

- DIAN registry
- Annual documentation
- Institutional reports

Documents should be organized by year.

## Donation Information

Information explaining how people can support the foundation.

Examples:

- Bank details
- Donation campaigns
- Contact information

---

# Usability Principles

Because the platform will be used by non-technical staff, the system must prioritize:

- Simplicity
- Clear navigation
- Minimal steps to publish content
- Easy media uploads
- Clear editing interfaces

The administration panel should allow staff to manage the site without developer assistance.
